# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- Developers, students, and self-learners tracking their daily revision and spaced repetition progress.
- Individuals needing a structured, aesthetic dashboard to guide their daily learning workflows.

## Product Purpose
- To provide a frictionless, vibrant, and highly engaging dashboard to track spaced repetition schedules.
- To reduce cognitive load by highlighting exactly what needs to be reviewed today.
- Success is a daily companion app that feels lightning-fast, simple, and encourages daily habit completion.

## Brand Personality
- Vibrant, high-contrast, playful, modern, tech-forward (inspired by Stripe, Vercel, Supabase).
- Energetic yet clean, using bold colors for state indication.

## Anti-references
- Heavy glassmorphism, floating cards with huge shadows, or complex page-load animations that distract from the learning task.

## Design Principles
- **Tech-Forward Playfulness**: Engage users with vibrant color accents and high-fidelity typography, making tracking feel active and rewarding.
- **Structured Rhythm**: Use clean, outlined containers and precise borders instead of muddy shadows and floating elements to maintain order.
- **Frictionless Workflow**: Prioritize lightning-fast page interactions and simple layout navigation to keep the focus entirely on study.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios >= 4.5:1 for standard body text.
- Supports prefers-reduced-motion media queries and respects screen reader accessibility.
